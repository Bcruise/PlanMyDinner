Butter Chicken

Ingredients

2 large chicken breasts 1 ¼ cups panko bread crumbs ¼ cup parmesan cheese grated 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 egg salt & pepper to taste ⅓ cup vegetable oil

Instructions

STEP 1 Freeze chicken breasts about 20 minutes or so.
STEP 2 Cut chicken breasts in half horizontally and pound to ¼" thickness.
STEP 3 Combine panko bread crumbs, parmesan cheese and garlic powder in a small shallow dish.
STEP 4 Whisk egg in a separate bowl. Dip chicken in egg and then in the crumb mixture pressing to adhere.
STEP 5 Preheat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Gently add chicken and cook 2-3 minutes per side or until golden brown and chicken reaches 165°F.
